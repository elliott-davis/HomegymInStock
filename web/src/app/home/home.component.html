Total Subscribers {{totalSubscribers}} help FAQ How do I subscribe to new notifications?

Make sure you log in (top right link). From there, click items in the left most menu. Filter by items you want at the top, then click the subscribe button on the right side of the table. This will save your subscription and send you an email the next time the item is in stock

 How can I request new items be added?

For now, this is still manual. Please submit a issue at Homegym In Stock. Currently the site is only configured to scrape Rogue and Ironmaster, but I'm hoping to add more soon.
